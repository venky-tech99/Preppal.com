Analogy - Logical Reasoning

What is Analogy?

An Analogy is a comparison between two things that are alike in some way, often used to explain a concept or solve reasoning problems. In logical reasoning, analogy questions test your ability to identify relationships between pairs of words, numbers, shapes, or ideas.

Example: Bird : Nest :: Bee : Hive Here, "Bird" is related to "Nest" as "Bee" is related to "Hive" — both show a living creature and its dwelling place.
