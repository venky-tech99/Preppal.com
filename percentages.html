Quantitative Aptitude - Percentages

Theory: Understanding Percentages

  • Definition: A percentage is a number or ratio expressed as a fraction of 100.
  • Calculation: (Part / Whole) × 100
  • Applications: Used in statistics, surveys, finance, and more.
  • Proportions Comparison: Compare different quantities easily.
  • Visualizing: Use pie charts and bar graphs.
  • Common Errors: Misinterpreting percentage increases/decreases.
  • Change Importance: Understand trends and variations.
  • Surveys/Polls: Used for public opinion analysis.
  • Limitations: Doesn’t show actual quantity.
