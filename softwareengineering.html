Introduction

Software Engineering is the systematic approach to the development, operation, maintenance, and retirement of software. It applies engineering principles to software development to produce reliable, efficient, and maintainable software products.

Why is Software Engineering important?
  • Helps manage large and complex software systems
  • Ensures quality, reliability, and maintainability
  • Reduces development cost and time
  • Improves team collaboration and productivity
