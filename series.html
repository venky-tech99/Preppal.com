Series - Logical Reasoning

What is a Series?

In logical reasoning, a series is a sequence of numbers, letters, or figures that follow a certain pattern or rule. The objective is to identify the rule and determine the missing or next term. Series can be numerical, alphabetical, or mixed, and are a common type of reasoning question in competitive exams.
